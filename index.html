💡 Why Pre-AI Grounding Matters

Before you interact with AI, it's crucial to establish YOUR context, thinking, and evaluation criteria. This helps you:

  • Evaluate AI outputs critically - You'll know what to check and verify
  • Ask better questions - Your prompts become specific and targeted
  • Learn meaningfully - AI enhances your understanding rather than replacing it
  • Set success criteria - You'll know when you've actually learned, not just consumed information
  • Avoid passive consumption - You remain an active, critical thinker
